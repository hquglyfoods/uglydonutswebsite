We believe the food you remember most
is the food someone made for you.

That is why our founder started Ugly Donuts & Corn Dogs. As a boy in Korea, his mother would buy him a corn dog from a small stand on the way home. It was warm. It was hers. That single memory is what we are trying to give back, one warm corn dog at a time.
