Good food should be easy for everyone to find and enjoy, and that includes the people who use our website. We are committed to making uglydonutsncorndogs.com accessible to as many people as possible, regardless of ability or the technology they use.

Our commitment

We want every guest to be able to browse our menu, find a location, apply for a job, and reach out to us without barriers. Accessibility is not a one-time project for us. It is part of how we think about building and maintaining our website.

Standards we follow

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at Level AA. These guidelines, published by the World Wide Web Consortium, are an internationally recognized standard for making web content more accessible to people with a wide range of disabilities, including visual, auditory, motor, and cognitive disabilities.

What we have done

Steps we have taken to make our site more accessible include:

  • Providing a "Skip to content" link so keyboard users can move past navigation
  • Using clear heading structure so the page is easy to navigate with assistive technology
  • Associating form labels with their fields so forms work with screen readers
  • Choosing text and background colors that meet recognized contrast ratios for readability
  • Providing descriptive alternative text for meaningful images
  • Designing the site to work across screen sizes and on mobile devices

An ongoing effort

We continue to review and improve the accessibility of our website over time. Some content may not yet fully meet our goals, and accessibility can also depend on the specific browser, device, or assistive technology you use. If you encounter something that is difficult to use, we want to hear about it so we can address it.

Report a barrier

If you have trouble accessing any part of our website, please let us know. When you contact us, it helps to include:

  • The page or feature where you experienced the problem
  • A short description of what happened
  • The browser, device, or assistive technology you were using, if you know it

We will do our best to provide the information or service you need through an alternative method, and to fix the issue where we can.
