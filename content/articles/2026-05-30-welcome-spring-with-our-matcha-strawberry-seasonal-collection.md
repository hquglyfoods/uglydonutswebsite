Spring is a season of fresh starts, vibrant colors, and simple moments worth savoring. Inspired by the bright flavors of the season, we’re excited to introduce two limited-time creations that bring together the perfect balance of premium matcha and sweet strawberry: the Matcha Strawberry Blossom Latte and the Strawberry Matcha Donut.

Crafted with quality ingredients and made to celebrate the arrival of warmer days, this seasonal collection is available for a limited time at Ugly Donuts & Corn Dogs.

## Matcha Strawberry Blossom Latte

Our Matcha Strawberry Blossom Latte starts with ceremonial-grade matcha, considered the highest quality matcha available and prized for its vibrant color, smooth texture, and rich, naturally complex flavor.

Rather than relying on artificial flavorings, we pair our matcha with a freshly made strawberry cloud foam crafted in-house using our premium strawberry syrup. Made without artificial flavors or colors, the cloud foam delivers a naturally sweet strawberry flavor and a velvety texture that perfectly complements the matcha beneath it.

To finish, we top each drink with freeze-dried strawberries, adding an extra layer of real strawberry flavor and a beautiful finishing touch.

The result is a refreshing balance of earthy ceremonial matcha, creamy strawberry foam, and bright strawberry notes in every sip.

## Strawberry Matcha Donut

Our Strawberry Matcha Donut is inspired by the same commitment to quality ingredients and balanced flavors.

We begin with our signature fluffy donut and layer it with our premium strawberry syrup, fresh sliced strawberries, and a light, creamy matcha cream made with ceremonial-grade matcha.

Each ingredient has a purpose. The strawberry syrup provides sweetness and depth, the fresh strawberries bring natural brightness and texture, and the smooth matcha cream adds richness while highlighting the distinctive flavor of premium matcha.

The result is a donut that feels indulgent yet balanced, allowing both the strawberry and matcha to shine.

## Made for a Limited Time

Seasonal offerings are our way of celebrating the flavors and moments that make each season special. Our Matcha Strawberry Blossom Latte and Strawberry Matcha Donut are available for a limited time this spring, making now the perfect time to try them before they’re gone.

Join us in welcoming the season with a collection that highlights one of the world’s most beloved teas and the sweetness of fresh strawberry in a uniquely Ugly way.
