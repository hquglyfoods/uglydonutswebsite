Our Pearland store on Broadway Street brings Korean corn dogs and handmade donuts to the Houston area. Every corn dog is battered and fried to order, and every donut is finished by hand.

## The neighborhood
Pearland is one of the fastest growing communities in the Houston area, with Broadway Street as one of its main corridors of shopping and dining. The store sits close to Pearland Town Center and is an easy stop for families across the south Houston suburbs.

## Parking and getting here
The store offers convenient parking, with easy access from Broadway Street and nearby State Highway 288.

## Why visit this location
It is a fresh, made to order treat for a shopping trip, a family outing, or an everyday craving. Guest favorites here include our Hot Cheetos Corn Dog and Potato Corn Dog, fried fresh the moment you order.
