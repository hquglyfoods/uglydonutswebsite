Our Forest Hills store is on Austin Street, the main shopping and dining stretch of this Queens neighborhood. It is a standalone shop where every corn dog is fried to order and every donut is finished by hand.

## The neighborhood
Forest Hills is one of the most charming neighborhoods in Queens, known for its tree lined streets, Tudor style homes, and the lively Austin Street corridor. Forest Hills Stadium, a longtime concert and tennis venue, sits nearby and brings visitors to the area through the season.

## Parking and getting here
The store is a short walk from the 71st and Continental Avenue subway station, served by several lines. Street parking and nearby lots are available, and Austin Street is comfortable to explore on foot.

## Why visit this location
It is an easy stop while shopping or dining on Austin Street, before a show at Forest Hills Stadium, or on a walk through the neighborhood. Every order is made fresh, the same way we make it across all of our stores.
