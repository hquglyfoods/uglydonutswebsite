Our flagship store sits on the second level of American Dream in East Rutherford, one of the largest retail and entertainment complexes in the country. It is the home of our full menu, where every corn dog is battered and fried to order and every donut is finished by hand.

## The neighborhood
American Dream brings together Nickelodeon Universe, the DreamWorks Water Park, an indoor ski slope, shopping, and dining under one roof, drawing visitors from across the New York and New Jersey area. The complex sits beside MetLife Stadium, just off Route 3 and the New Jersey Turnpike.

## Parking and getting here
The complex offers extensive on-site parking, with garages connected directly to the mall. We are on Level 2. From the parking decks, follow the signs toward the dining and entertainment areas.

## Why visit this location
This is where the brand began and where you will find the widest selection. It is an easy stop before or after a day at the theme parks, a game at MetLife Stadium, or a shopping trip, and it is built to handle a crowd while keeping every order made fresh.
